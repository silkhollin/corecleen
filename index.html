About CoreCleen

Corecleen aims to build and maintain long-term client relationships, with a competitive and excellent cleaning service.
The commitment to excellence and professionalism by our cleaning team is why we have earned the loyalty
and trust of our regular customers and are the best choice for new clients.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with top-quality professional and friendly cleaning services that supersede their expectations. By making the interests of our clients our number one priority we will earn their trust and loyalty and they will become our lifelong customers

Our Vision

To continually provide excellent services that will propel us to become a household name within the cleaning industry in Uganda

Our Values

Service Excellence - Taking responsibility to complete your tasks, to perform the duties required by your job and to be present for your shifts in order to fulfill and further the goals of the company.

Respect - We respect the individual, and believe that individuals who are treated with respect and given responsibility respond by giving their best.

Accountability - We own problems and we are always responsive.

Reliability - Being punctual and dependable resulting in being committed to the team, our customers and the company.

Team Work - Increased speed, shared satisfaction, creative solutions and working together.

Customer Goals

  • Provide the highest standard of service and workmanship
  • Build long-term relationships with customers through quality service at competitive prices
  • Maintain integrity and initiative
  • Professionalism and pride in our market position
  • Long-term stability
  • Maintain a well-trained and motivated staff
